The Urge to Check Social Media

In a way, checking social media over
and over again is a form of attention-
seeking, Dr. Larry Rosen told Refinery.
Waiting for a like, comment, or any
other thing that allows you to validate
yourself in front of a large audience is
something that most people desire.
